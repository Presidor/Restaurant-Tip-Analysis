# 🍽️ Restaurant Tip Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project explores tipping behavior in a restaurant setting using exploratory data analysis (EDA). The goal is to uncover patterns in customer behavior, tipping trends, and operational insights that can help improve restaurant performance and decision-making.

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains **244 records** with the following features:

* `total_bill` – Total bill amount
* `tip` – Tip given by the customer
* `sex` – Gender of the customer
* `smoker` – Smoking status
* `day` – Day of visit
* `time` – Meal time (Lunch/Dinner)
* `size` – Number of people at the table

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Billing & Tipping

* Tips range from **$1 to $10** 
* Strong positive correlation between **total bill and tip (~0.68)** 
* Larger bills generally result in higher tips

---

### 👥 Customer Behavior

* More **male customers** than female 
* Majority of customers are **non-smokers** 
* **Table size of 2** is the most common 

---

### 📅 Time & Day Trends

* **Weekends (Sat & Sun)** have higher customer turnout 
* **Dinner time dominates** over lunch in both sales and tips 
* Tips during dinner are **~3× higher than lunch** 

---

### 🚬 Smoker vs Non-Smoker

* Non-smokers contribute **higher total tips** 
* Only Friday shows relatively higher smoker presence

---

### 📊 Revenue Insights

* Higher revenue generated on **weekends** 
* **Dinner service** generates the most income

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

* Focus staffing and resources on **weekend dinners**
* Optimize seating for **tables of 2**
* Enhance customer experience during dinner service
* Consider targeted promotions for weekday traffic
